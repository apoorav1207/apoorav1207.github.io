01 — About

Building Vision
for Machines

I am Apoorav Maulik Sharma, a former Research Fellow in the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) at the University Institute of Engineering & Technology (UIET), Panjab University, Chandigarh.

My research bridges mathematical foundations and practical imaging systems — specializing in multimodal medical image denoising, low-light image fusion (infrared & visible spectrum), image inpainting, and image matting. I work with techniques spanning spatial-domain filtering, wavelet transforms, Bayesian analysis, and deep learning architectures.

With 19 peer-reviewed publications, 543+ citations, and features in Laser Focus World and Nature Scientific Reports, I am dedicated to research that transforms diagnostic imaging and visual AI.
